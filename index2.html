Indian Institute of Technology Bombay

Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, located in Powai, Mumbai, is one of the premier engineering institutions in India.

  • Location: Powai, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
  • Establishment: 1958
  • Academic Programs: Undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, science, design, and management.
  • Departments: Aerospace Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, etc.
  • Research: Renowned for research contributions in nanotechnology, biotechnology, information technology, and sustainable development.
  • Rankings: Consistently ranks among the top engineering institutions in India and globally recognized for academic excellence.
  • Campus: Spread over 550 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including laboratories, libraries, sports complexes, and residential accommodations.
  • Student Life: Vibrant student community with numerous clubs, societies, and cultural events. Annual festivals include Mood Indigo (cultural) and Techfest (technical).
  • Alumni: Impressive alumni network with graduates making significant contributions globally.
